How to Play

  1. Choose a Category: Select Chemistry, Capitals, or German to set the theme and content for the cards.
  2. Flip Cards: Click (or use Enter/Space with keyboard navigation) on a card to reveal its content.
  3. Find Matches: Flip two cards at a time, trying to find pairs. Matching cards remain face-up; non-matching flip back after a short delay.
  4. Track Your Stats: Watch your moves, matches, and time in the stats bar above.
  5. Use the Controls: Change category any time or use Reset to start fresh.
  6. Win the Game: Match all pairs with as few moves and in the shortest time possible.
  7. Accessibility: Fully keyboard-accessible and designed with high colour contrast and emoji/image fallbacks.
